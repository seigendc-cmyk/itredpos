# SCI POS Business Intelligence Control Centre

## Purpose

The Business Intelligence Control Centre is the master BI page inside SCI POS.

It must make business intelligence understandable to ordinary vendors while still providing deep analytical power for owners, managers, and supervisors.

The Control Centre must behave like a business command centre and a searchable intelligence book.

## Core Principle

The user must be able to search, filter, understand, and act.

The control flow is:

Search
to
Filter
to
Analyze
to
Understand
to
Act

## Main Sections

The Control Centre must include:

- Landing Dashboard
- Daily Business Brief
- Stock Integrity

- Cash Control
- Staff Intelligence
- Sales Intelligence

The landing dashboard must show:

- Business Health Score
- Protected Profit
- Stock Risk
- COGS Risk
- Cash Risk
- Staff Risk
- Supplier Risk
- Delivery Risk
- Customer Risk
- Branch Risk
- Critical Alerts
- Recommended Actions

## Book-Like Intelligence Logic

The BI page must feel like a business intelligence book.

Each chapter must explain one business domain.

The user should be able to open any chapter in any order.

Each chapter must include:

- Summary
- Key Metrics
- Trends
- Risks
- Alerts
- Recommendations
- Investigation Links

## Search Logic

Users must search naturally.

Examples:

- Show stock variance this month
- Show products sold below cost
- Show shelves to count today
- Show high-risk cashiers
- Show supplier price increases
- Show delivery cash not remitted
- Show branches losing profit
- Show customers at risk

## Filters

Every BI chapter must support filters:

- Period
- Branch
- Warehouse
- Shelf
- Product
- Category
- Staff
- Supplier
- Customer
- Driver
- Terminal
- Risk Level
- Alert Status

## Decision Feed

The Control Centre must provide a decision feed.

Each feed item must show:

Observation
Reason
Risk
Recommended Action
Expected Benefit

Example:

Observation:
Battery 70AH has repeated shortages.

Reason:
Three negative variances in 30 days.

Risk:
Possible stock leakage.

Recommended Action:
Run surprise shelf count and review staff movement logs.

## Role-Based Intelligence

Owner:
Full BI access.

Branch Manager:
Branch-level BI.

Warehouse Staff:
Stocktake tasks and variance tasks.

Cashier:
Personal performance and compliance feed.

Delivery Staff:
Delivery task and performance feed.

## Strategic Principle

The Business Intelligence Control Centre is the brain of SCI POS.

It must turn raw data into simple business decisions.

The user must leave the page knowing what to do next.
