# SCI POS Business Intelligence Profit Protection

## Purpose

Profit Protection is the ultimate objective of SCI POS Business Intelligence.

Businesses do not fail because they lack sales reports.

Businesses fail because profit is silently destroyed through:

- Stock theft
- Stock leakage
- Weak margins
- Poor purchasing decisions
- Supplier cost increases
- Uncontrolled discounts
- Refund abuse
- Cash shortages
- Delivery leakage
- Dead stock
- Overstocking
- Stockouts
- Operational inefficiencies

SCI POS must continuously identify and quantify threats to profit.

---

## Core Principle

Revenue is not profit.

Profit is not cash.

SCI POS must continuously measure:

Sales
minus COGS
equals Gross Profit

Gross Profit
minus Stock Loss
minus Discount Loss
minus Refund Loss
minus Write-Off Loss
minus Cash Variance
minus Delivery Loss
equals Protected Profit

Protected Profit is the most important profitability measure in the system.

---

The BI engine must track:

## Protected Profit Calculation

The BI engine must calculate:

Gross Profit

minus

Stock Variance Value

minus

Write-Off Value

minus

Discount Leakage

minus

Refund Impact

minus

Cash Variance

equals

Protected Profit

Example:

Sales: 20,000

COGS: 14,800

Gross Profit: 5,200

Stock Loss: 780

Discount Leakage: 420

Refund Impact: 200

Cash Variance: 100

Protected Profit: 3,700

The owner must see how operational losses reduce profitability.
