# SCI Security Governance SOT

**Owner:** Digital Commerce (Private) Limited  
**Status:** Controlled Source of Truth  

## Security principles

SCI uses:

- Least privilege.
- Explicit authorization.
- Tenant isolation.
- Role-based access.
- Secure secrets management.
- Immutable audit history.
- Controlled recovery.

## Prohibited actions

- Committing production secrets.
- Bypassing authentication in production.
- Bypassing licensing.
- Client-controlled audit identity.
- Cross-tenant reads or writes.
- Silent deletion of stock or financial records.
- Unauthorized administrative actions.

## Severity levels

- CRITICAL
- HIGH
- MEDIUM
- LOW
- INFORMATIONAL
