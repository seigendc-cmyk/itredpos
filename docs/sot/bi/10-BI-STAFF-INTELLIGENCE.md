# SCI POS Business Intelligence Staff Intelligence

## Purpose

Staff Intelligence monitors accountability, performance, compliance, risk, and operational discipline.

SCI POS must not only track what staff sold.

It must understand how each staff member affects stock integrity, cash control, profit protection, customer service, delivery fulfilment, and business risk.

The purpose is not to accuse staff automatically.

The purpose is to create visibility, accountability, coaching opportunities, and early risk detection.

## Core Principle

Every staff action must contribute to a staff intelligence profile.

Each staff member must have:

- Performance Score
- Trust Score
- Cash Risk Score
- Stock Risk Score
- Compliance Score
- Approval Dependency Score
- Customer Handling Score

## Staff Activity Inputs

SCI POS must collect:

- Login activity
- Logout activity
- Shift activity
- Sales processed
- Refunds processed
- Voids processed
- Discounts applied
- Price overrides requested
- Stock counts performed
- Stock adjustments submitted
- Approvals requested
- Delivery actions
- Cash drawer activity
- Cash variance history
- Stock variance links

## Cashier Intelligence

Cashiers must be scored on:

- Sales volume
- Transaction accuracy
- Void frequency
- Refund frequency
- Discount frequency
- Price override frequency
- Cash variance
- Drawer openings
- Suspended transactions
- Transaction completion discipline

Example:

Cashier: John
Cashier Score: 62 out of 100
Risk Level: High
Reasons:
Repeated discounts, cash variance, and excessive voids.

## Warehouse Staff Intelligence

Warehouse staff must be scored on:

- Stock receiving accuracy
- Stock transfer accuracy
- Stock count accuracy
- Adjustment frequency
- Shelf discipline
- Variance links
- Count task completion
- Receiving compliance

Example:

Warehouse Staff: Peter
Stock Integrity Score: 74 out of 100
Issue:
Repeated variance after stock receiving tasks.

## Manager Intelligence

Managers must be scored on:

- Approval quality
- Variance resolution
- Branch discipline
- Staff supervision
- Cash control enforcement
- Stocktake completion
- Alert resolution
- Policy compliance

## Staff Risk Detection

SCI POS must detect:

- Staff linked to repeated stock variances
- Staff linked to repeated cash variances
- Staff performing excessive voids
- Staff processing abnormal refunds
- Staff applying unusual discounts
- Staff requesting many overrides
- Staff approving risky transactions
- Staff repeatedly working high-loss shifts
- Staff delaying shift closure
- Staff ignoring count tasks

## Staff Trust Score

The Staff Trust Score must combine:

- Cash discipline
- Stock discipline
- Transaction discipline
- Approval behavior
- Compliance history
- Risk event frequency
- Resolution history

Risk scores should not permanently punish staff.

Scores must improve gradually when behavior improves.

## Role-Based BI Feed

Staff must only see BI feeds relevant to their role.

Cashiers see personal sales, transaction quality, cash discipline, and basic performance.

Warehouse users see stock counts, stock accuracy, receiving tasks, and variance tasks.

Managers see branch staff performance, risk summaries, alerts, and approval tasks.

Owners see full staff intelligence across all branches.

## Staff Alerts

SCI POS must create alerts for:

- High cash risk staff
- High stock risk staff
- Excessive voids
- Excessive refunds
- Excessive discounts
- Repeated stock count errors
- Suspicious approval activity
- Staff linked to repeated variance
- Staff ignoring assigned BI tasks

## Future Prediction Possibilities

SCI POS must be ready to predict:

- Staff likely to create cash variance
- Staff linked to future stock risk
- Staff requiring training
- Staff likely to misuse discounts
- Staff performance decline
- Staff trust improvement
- Branch risk caused by staff behavior

## Strategic Principle

Staff Intelligence must create a culture of accountability.

The system must help business owners manage staff using evidence, not guesswork.

SCI POS must support fair review, coaching, investigation, and control.
