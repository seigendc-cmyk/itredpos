# SCI POS Business Intelligence Sales Intelligence

## Purpose

Sales Intelligence transforms raw sales transactions into business insight.

Most POS systems show sales totals.

SCI POS must explain:

- What sold
- Why it sold
- Who sold it
- Where it sold
- When it sold
- Whether it was profitable
- Whether sales are sustainable
- What future sales risks exist

Sales Intelligence must support operational decisions, purchasing decisions, pricing decisions, stock decisions, staffing decisions, and growth decisions.

---

## Core Principle

Sales volume alone is not a measure of business success.

SCI POS must always analyze:

Sales
plus Gross Profit
plus Stock Availability
plus Customer Behavior
plus Margin Quality
plus Risk Exposure

Sales Intelligence must connect revenue to profitability.

---

## Sales Analysis Dimensions

The BI layer must analyze sales by:

### Product

- Best sellers
- Slow movers
- Dead stock
- Revenue contribution
- Gross profit contribution
- Margin quality

### Category

- Category revenue
- Category growth
- Category profitability
- Category stock turnover

### Branch

- Branch sales
- Branch growth
- Branch profitability
- Branch sales risk

### Staff

- Sales by cashier
- Sales by salesperson
- Conversion performance
- Discount activity

### Customer

- Customer purchases
- Customer value
- Customer frequency
- Customer churn risk

### Time

- Hourly sales
- Daily sales
- Weekly sales
- Monthly sales
- Seasonal patterns

---

## Product Sales Intelligence

Every product must show:

- Units sold
- Revenue generated
- Gross profit generated
- Margin percentage
- Stock turnover
- Days of stock cover
- Stockout history
- Return frequency
- Refund impact

Example:

Product: Battery 70AH

Units Sold: 120

Revenue: 4200

Gross Profit: 1100

Margin: 26%

Stock Turnover: High

Risk: Repeated stock variance

---

## Sales Velocity Intelligence

SCI POS must calculate:

- Daily sales velocity
- Weekly sales velocity
- Monthly sales velocity
- Seasonal velocity

The BI layer must identify:

- Fast movers
- Medium movers
- Slow movers
- Dead stock

This data feeds purchasing and stocktake intelligence.

---

## Sales Opportunity Detection

The system must identify:

- Growing products
- Growing categories
- Growing branches
- Emerging customer demand
- Cross-selling opportunities
- Upselling opportunities

Example:

Demand for brake pads increased 18% over 60 days.

Recommendation:
Increase stock cover and review supplier capacity.

---

## Sales Risk Detection

The BI engine must detect:

- Sales decline
- Stockout-related sales loss
- Margin decline
- Product dependency risk
- Branch underperformance
- Customer concentration risk
- Sales created through excessive discounts
- Revenue growth without profit growth

---

## Sales Forecasting

The system must forecast:

- Expected daily sales
- Expected weekly sales
- Expected monthly sales
- Product demand
- Category demand
- Seasonal demand

Forecasts must explain:

- Why the prediction exists
- Historical basis
- Risk factors
- Confidence level

---

## Sales Alerts

SCI POS must create alerts for:

- Product sales collapse
- Category decline
- Branch sales decline
- Revenue growth with weak margin
- Fast mover approaching stockout
- Product demand spike
- Sales anomaly
- Excessive discount-driven sales

---

## Sales Recommendations

Examples:

Observation:
Brake Pad sales increased 18%.

Risk:
Current stock will be depleted in 6 days.

Recommendation:
Increase reorder quantity.

---

Observation:
Cooking Oil sales declining.

Reason:
Competitor pricing suspected.

Recommendation:
Review pricing strategy.

---

## Future Prediction Possibilities

SCI POS must be ready to predict:

- Future demand
- Product popularity shifts
- Category growth
- Seasonal demand cycles
- Branch demand patterns
- Customer purchase cycles
- Revenue risk

---

## Strategic Principle

Sales Intelligence must help the owner understand not only what was sold, but what should be stocked, priced, promoted, protected, and planned next.

Revenue without intelligence creates uncertainty.

Revenue with intelligence creates growth.
