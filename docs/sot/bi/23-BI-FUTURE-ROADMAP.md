# SCI POS Business Intelligence Future Roadmap

## Purpose

The BI Future Roadmap defines how SCI POS Business Intelligence must evolve over time.

SCI POS must begin with strong rule-based intelligence and gradually mature into predictive, adaptive, and AI-assisted decision intelligence.

The roadmap must protect the current production build while allowing future expansion.

## Phase 1: Rule-Based BI Foundation

Initial BI must focus on:

- Event logging
- Audit trails
- Stock integrity
- Stocktake intelligence
- Theft detection
- COGS intelligence
- Profit protection
- Cash control
- Staff intelligence
- Alerts
- Recommendations

This phase must use clear business rules that can be tested, explained, and trusted.

## Phase 2: Advanced Stock and Profit Intelligence

Add deeper intelligence for:

- Shelf risk scoring
- Product theft risk scoring
- Protected profit calculations
- Margin leakage analysis
- Supplier cost impact
- Branch stock integrity comparison
- High-risk stocktake scheduling
- Continuous stock watchdog logic

## Phase 3: Predictive Intelligence

Introduce predictive models for:

- Stockout prediction
- Theft risk prediction
- Margin collapse prediction
- Supplier risk prediction
- Customer churn prediction
- Branch decline prediction
- Cash variance prediction
- Delivery failure prediction

Predictions must always explain their reasoning.

## Phase 4: BI Control Centre Expansion

Expand the Business Intelligence Control Centre with:

- Natural search
- Advanced filters
- BI chapters
- Investigation workflows
- Daily business brief
- Owner action list
- Role-based dashboards
- Exportable reports
- WhatsApp owner summaries

## Phase 5: AI-Assisted Decision Intelligence

Future AI may assist with:

- Plain-language business summaries
- Pattern explanation
- Recommendation prioritization
- Risk investigation summaries
- Supplier comparison
- Stock planning
- Customer retention suggestions

AI must not replace audit trails, rules, or evidence.

AI must explain business logic clearly.

## Future Possibilities

SCI POS may later support:

- Industry-specific BI templates
- Supplier benchmarking
- Product demand forecasting
- Auto reorder recommendations
- Branch expansion recommendations
- Delivery route optimization
- Voice BI summaries
- Mobile owner alerts
- Smart approval workflows
- Business health benchmarking

## Strategic Principle

SCI POS Business Intelligence must evolve without losing trust.

The system must remain explainable, auditable, role-based, and useful to ordinary vendors.

The long-term goal is to make SCI POS a Business Operating System for African commerce.
