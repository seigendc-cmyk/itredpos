# SCI POS Business Intelligence Data Collection

## Purpose

The quality of Business Intelligence depends entirely on the quality of collected data.

SCI POS must collect complete operational, financial, inventory, delivery, customer, supplier, and staff activity data.

The BI layer must never depend on summaries alone.

All intelligence must be derived from raw business events.

---

## Core Data Sources

SCI POS shall collect data from:

### Sales

- Sales Transactions
- Line Items
- Discounts
- Refunds
- Voids
- Returns
- Payment Methods

### Inventory

- Stock Receiving
- Stock Transfers
- Stock Adjustments
- Stock Write-Offs
- Stock Counts
- Variances
- Shelf Movements

### Purchasing

- Purchase Orders
- Supplier Invoices
- Goods Received Notes
- Cost Changes

### Cash

- Cash Drawer Activity
- Shift Activity
- Cash Variances
- Cash Remittances

### Staff

- Login Activity
- Logout Activity
- Approvals
- Overrides
- Role Changes

### Delivery

- Delivery Requests
- Driver Assignment
- GPS Activity
- Delivery Confirmation
- Secret Code Verification
- Cash Collection
